Food safety

At Shef, we are committed to helping ensure that your
food will always be safe to eat. All shefs are required to
pass an accredited food safety certification exam and
comply with all local laws and regulations. In regions
that have not yet implemented home cooking laws,
shefs are required to cook out of commercial kitchens
or other legally permissible facilities.
